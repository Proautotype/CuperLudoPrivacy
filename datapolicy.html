Thank you for playing Cuper Ludo! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data We Collect

Currently, Cuper Ludo does not collect personal data (e.g., name, email) directly. However, the game includes third-party services that may collect anonymized data for advertising and analytics.

2. Unity Ads

Cuper Ludo uses Unity Ads to display advertisements. Unity Technologies may collect and process data to deliver personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Gameplay interactions with ads
  • Approximate location (non-precise)

For details, review Unity's Privacy Policy.

3. Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized ads from Unity by:

  • Android: Enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalization" in Google Settings.
  • iOS: Enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" in Privacy Settings.

4. Analytics & Crash Data

We may use Unity Analytics (anonymous data) to improve the game, such as:

  • Session length
  • Crash reports
  • Feature usage

5. Children's Privacy

Cuper Ludo does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Ads shown to children (if applicable) comply with COPPA.

6. Data Security

We implement safeguards to protect data, but no method is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted here. Continued use of the game implies acceptance.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your region (e.g., GDPR in the EU), you may request:

  • Access to your data
